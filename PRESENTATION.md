# The Catch: Restaurant Ordering Platform

## Presentation for Stakeholders

---

## Slide 1: Platform Overview

### "The Catch: A Complete Restaurant Ordering Platform"

**What It Is:**
A custom-built online ordering system designed specifically for The Catch seafood restaurants.

**Who It Serves:**
- **Customers** — Order food online for pickup, delivery, or dine-in
- **Restaurant Managers** — Update menus, prices, and location settings
- **Kitchen Staff** — See and manage incoming orders in real-time

**Current Scale:**
- Supports 17+ restaurant locations across Texas and Oklahoma
- Single platform manages all locations from one place

---

## Slide 2: Customer Experience

### "Order Online in Minutes"

**What Customers Can Do:**

- **Browse menus by location** — Each restaurant can have different prices and available items
- **Customize orders** — Choose sizes, sides, add-ons, and special instructions
- **Order their way** — Pickup, delivery, or dine-in options
- **Check out as a guest** — No account or login required
- **Track their order** — See when it's confirmed, being prepared, and ready

**How It Looks:**
- Works on phones, tablets, and computers
- Clean, modern design that matches The Catch brand
- Fast loading — pages appear in under one second

---

## Slide 3: Restaurant Management

### "Control Everything from One Dashboard"

**What Managers Can Update:**

- **Menu Items** — Names, descriptions, photos, and prices
- **Categories** — Organize items into groups (Starters, Baskets, Combos, etc.)
- **Customization Options** — Size choices, dressings, sides, and add-ons
- **Item Badges** — Mark items as Spicy, Vegetarian, Gluten-Free, etc.
- **Location Settings** — Hours, phone numbers, addresses, and ordering availability

**Flexible Pricing:**
- Set a base price for each item
- Override prices at specific locations when needed
- Turn items on or off at individual locations

**No Technical Skills Required:**
- User-friendly editing interface (Sanity Studio)
- Changes appear on the website automatically

---

## Slide 4: Kitchen Operations

### "Kitchen Display System — Orders at a Glance"

**What Kitchen Staff See:**

- **Real-time order board** organized into columns:
  - New Orders (just placed)
  - In Progress (being prepared)
  - Ready (waiting for pickup)

- **Order details** including:
  - Customer name and contact info
  - All items with customizations
  - Special instructions
  - Time since order was placed

**Designed for Kitchen Use:**
- Runs on tablets (can be mounted in the kitchen)
- Dark color scheme that's easy on the eyes
- Sound alerts when new orders arrive
- Large text and buttons for quick updates

---

## Slide 5: Business Benefits

### "Why This Platform Matters"

**Direct Customer Relationships:**
- No fees to third-party ordering apps (like DoorDash or UberEats)
- The Catch owns all customer data and order history
- Build direct communication channels with customers

**Operational Efficiency:**
- One system for all 17+ locations
- Real-time kitchen updates reduce errors and wait times
- Complete order records for accounting and analysis

**Cost Transparency:**
- Every order captures exact prices at time of purchase
- Track delivery fees, taxes, and platform costs
- Per-location reporting capabilities

---

## Slide 6: Future Capabilities

### "What's Possible Next"

**Ready to Implement:**

- **Loyalty Program** — Reward repeat customers with points or discounts
- **Catering Orders** — Large orders with advance scheduling
- **Group Ordering** — Let multiple people add to one order
- **Scheduled Orders** — Place orders ahead of time for a specific pickup window
- **Customer Accounts** — Save favorites, view order history, faster checkout

**Additional Enhancements:**

- **Text Message (SMS) Updates** — Notify customers when orders are ready
- **Email Confirmations** — Automatic receipts and order summaries
- **Allergen Information** — Display allergy warnings on menu items
- **Nutrition Facts** — Calorie and ingredient details
- **Gift Card System** — Digital gift cards for online orders
- **In-Store Digital Menus** — Display menus on TVs inside restaurants

**Point of Sale Integration:**
- Connect with Revel (the restaurant's cash register system)
- Sync online orders directly to kitchen printers
- Unified reporting across online and in-store sales
